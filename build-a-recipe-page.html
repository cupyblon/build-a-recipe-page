Ribeye Steak fire grilled

How to make the best Wood-fire grilled steak

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lb Ribeye steak
  • 5 ml coarse seal salt
  • Butter
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Start wood fire and allow to burn down to gray and red coals.
  2. Place Ribeye steak on cutting board and sprinkel both sides in coarse sea salt.Aprox 45-60 mins
  3. Place Ribeye Steak indirectly over coals, flip every 10 minutes until internal center tempature in 140 degrees F
  4. Place Ribeye steak on clean cutting board and allow to rest for 10 minutes before cutting
